3. **Misleading Use**: Suggesting endorsement or affiliation

4. **Confusing Similarity**: Using similar names that cause confusion

## Guidelines for Use

### When Using Our Trademarks:

1. **Use as Adjective**: Use as an adjective, not a noun
   - ✅ "IronDAG blockchain"
   - ❌ "the IronDAG" (use "the IronDAG blockchain")

2. **Proper Attribution**: Include proper attribution
   - ✅ "IronDAG is a trademark of IronDAG Protocol"

3. **No Modification**: Don't modify or combine with other marks
   - ❌ "IronDAGCoin"
   - ❌ "IDAG Token" (if referring to a different token)

4. **Distinctive Styling**: Use distinctive styling when possible

   - ✅ Use proper capitalization: "IronDAG" not "irondag"
